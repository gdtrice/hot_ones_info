S1 - Ep7 Anthony Rizzo On Chicago Cubs Rivalries & Baseball Superstitions While Eating Spicy Wings

Date Aired: Tue, May 12, 15



Chicago Cubs first baseman Anthony Rizzo is a legitimate powerhouse on the baseball diamond—a 6 foot 3, 240 pound all-star who knocked in 32 home runs last year. It's obvious he can handle the fastball—but what about the heat from a cayenne pepper sauce? In "Hot Ones," we test Rizzo's limits by asking him progressively tougher questions paired with progressively spicier wings. Check out the video above to see him duke it out with host Sean Evans while discussing trash-talk from Cardinals fans, post-game fast-food munchies, and whether Slim Shady rocked bleached hair better than he did.
